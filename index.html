Frequently asked

The questions enterprise buyers ask first.

Direct answers from procurement, architecture, and security conversations we've had over the past 24 months.

What does it cost to engage Vervon for an Appian build?

Engagements typically start at AU$80K for a discovery and architecture sprint, with full builds priced against scope. We don't quote until we've understood your roadmap, regulatory context, and target SLAs — generic SI rate cards don't apply on Appian work.

How quickly can your engineers ramp on our platform?

A certified Appian engineer (L2 or L3) is productive on your codebase within 5 working days, assuming standard onboarding (access, environment, walkthrough). Our certification depth is the reason this is fast — not the reason it's slow.

Are your engineers Vervon-employed or sub-contracted?

All Vervon-employed. We don't sub-contract delivery. Your engagement contracts directly with Vervon Pty Ltd (Australia) or Vervon Technologies Pvt Ltd (India), depending on hub.

Do you support our compliance and security requirements?

Yes. We're ISO 27001:2022 certified, and our engineers are fluent in regulated-industry frameworks (APRA, RBI, GDPR, SOX). Compliance evidence and security documentation are available on request for procurement and audit teams.

What happens after the build goes live?

Managed Services takes over — 24/7 support across both hubs, enhancement queues, platform health monitoring, and quarterly architecture reviews. You don't get handed a manual and abandoned.

Can you embed engineers in our team instead of running an isolated project?

Yes — that's our Staff Augmentation track. Certified Appian developers embedded in your team, billed monthly, scaling up or down as your roadmap shifts.
